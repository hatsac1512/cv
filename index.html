My research lies at the field of change and innovation management in digitally mediated work. I examine when and how innovation achieves both performance and responsibility, particularly under conditions of technological and organizational complexity. I employ multilevel modelling, machine learning techniques and other qualitative methods to examine strategic processes in digitally mediated and cross-border contexts.
